The Kitty Hawk Air Society is a national AFJROTC academic honor society that recognizes academic and military achievement by cadets, provides additional leadership/educational opportunities, and encourages excellence. What it means to CA-935th is all that and more. To us, Kitty Hawk is a group of individuals trying to make a difference. In Kitty Hawk, we pledge to help our fellow cadets with their struggles in school as well as within the corps. We will help train and educate cadets in all areas needed and provide additional assistance to cadets in need of support. We will help tutor cadets after school as needed.
