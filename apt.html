Awareness Presentation Team (APT) consists of bright cadets passionate about spreading awareness to elementary/middle schoolers on several essential topics. We recognize that kids around this age may begin encountering new issues in their daily lives. Our team creates interactive slideshows featuring fun skits and audience questions centered around a set of topics.

Not only is APT beneficial to the kids we present to, but also allows us to help a community that has given so much to us while exploring public speaking. APT encourages teamwork, communication, and most importantly, pride in what we are able to accomplish. This year, APT is being led by c/2d Lt Siri Chiruta, c/2d Lt Kaitlyn Ly, and c/TSgt Zoya Qureshi.


For more APT media find us on Instagram!
