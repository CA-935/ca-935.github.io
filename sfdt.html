Saber Team is a group of dedicated and hard-working individuals who constantly hone their skills in fancy drill. Members work together to be an efficient, effective, and consistent drill team capable of facing any challenge. Incoming team members will be taught all necessary movements to perform alongside their teammates in competitions. They will be required to maintain discipline, coordination, and good work ethic that current members of Saber Team deem acceptable. This year the team is led by c/2d Lt. Jonathan Jiang and c/TSgt Caleb Shin.

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2021-2022 School Year:

Sweepstakes Saber Varsity (Golden Bear Nationals): 1st Place
Exhibition Saber Individual (Golden Bear Nationals): 1st Place
Exhibition Saber Four Man (Golden Bear Nationals): 1st place
Exhibition Saber Duel (Fontana): 1st Place
Exhibition Saber Duel (Golden Bear Nationals): 2nd Place
