Hello cadets, parents, staff, and visitors!

I'm Hilary Nguyen, the Group Commander for the 22-23 school year. I oversee the 8 other leaders on our unit's Senior Staff and work with our ASI and administration to continue our long-standing reputation of excellence. I'm thrilled for this chance to give back to this wonderful community, and I believe wholeheartedly that I will exit senior year a better person than I entered it. How could I not when I'm surrounded by extraordinary, inspiring cadets every day?

I knew stepping into 9th grade that I wanted to get to this position and excel within my other activities. So, I threw myself in headfirst. I joined our Kitty Hawk tutoring program. I learned how to pay respect to our flags through Flag Detail. I refined my public speaking skills through our Awareness Presentation Team. I led my flight to win RO Day—not once, but twice. My 9-year-old self never would have believed that I was able to balance so much.

That’s what I learned in high school: to figure it out, just go for it. Determination never failed me, but in the moments where I failed to determine my course forward, I had an amazing and supportive group of cadets around me to keep me going. Yes, I put in the work, but the cadets are my motivation for it all.

My goal is to encourage cadets to take advantage of the wealth of opportunities around them. There is so much that such a stellar program like this can provide for you. Everything and everyone you encounter holds a wealth of knowledge. You take a lesson, and you give your loyalty and respect in return. That’s how you become someone you’re proud of.

I cannot wait to seize this year and create an enriching experience for our cadets! Thank you for honoring me with your trust.

c/Col Hilary Nguyen
