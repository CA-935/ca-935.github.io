Youth Physical Fitness (YPF) is a competition that challenges an individual's physical prowess, as well as their ability to work in a team. It consists of a male and female team with 6 members each (including an alternate) from all the schools in the SDUSD Brigade. The events are the 20 meter crab walk, basketball throw, power throw, and a 400 meter relay race. The schools will compete against one another to score the most points at the competition to win 1st place. This year, YPF is led by c/2d Lt Tiendat Banh, and c/TSgt Gael Perez.
