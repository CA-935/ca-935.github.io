Armed Team requires a combination of strength, coordination, skill, discipline, timing, and consistency to perfect the movements that are taught. We work together as a family to teach all members of the team how to perform in sync with precision. It is required that incoming members of the armed fancy drill team would learn how to be fearless, and strong, and have the motivation to practice after school every day. Our instructors for the years 24-25 will be c/2d Lt Julian Schiff and c/TSgt Bridge Han.

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2023-2024 School Year:

Best Drilled Platoon (Brigade): 1st Place
Best Drilled Squadron (Brigade): 1st Place
LET 3 (BDI and Color Guard): 1st Place - Cameron Kim
Exhibition (Fontana): 1st Place
Regulation (Fontana): 1st Place
Novice Exhibition (Golden Bear): 1st Place
Novice Sweepstakes (Golden Bear): 1st Place
