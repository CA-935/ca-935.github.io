Stellar Xplorers is a team that competes in the StellarXplorers National Space Design Competition over the course of 4 months, at the very least. There are two types of rounds, Practice and Qualification Rounds. Points gained from Qualification Rounds contribute to the teamâ€™s overall score and will be the deciding factor on whether they progress into the Semi-Finals or not. Challenges include scenarios based on orbital mechanics and data analysis, which test skills in problem-solving, collaboration, and other areas. Over the past 6 years, our unit has triumphed in the Semi-Final and Final rounds. This year, c/2d Lt Heather Ngo and c/TSgt Sarah Zheng will lead the team to strive for a place in the Finals!

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2024-2025 School Year:

Qualification Round 1 (Nov. 3) - All three teams placed in the top 200 teams, Varsity taking 77th place nationally
Qualification Round 2 (Dec. 8) - All three teams placed in the top 150 teams, Varsity taking 39th place nationally
Qualification Round 3 (Jan. 25) - All three teams placed in the top 150 teams, JVA taking 17th place nationally
Junior Varsity A and Junior Varsity B both qualified for the Semi-Final Round (Feb. 14)
