Unarmed Fancy Drill Team is a complex drill team that consists of cadets using mainly their hands and feet to create unique sounds through stomps and slaps. Members of the team do not use any weapons or flags (hence the name Unarmed). However this doesn't stop them from being intense throughout the school year of competitions. Our Unarmed team has currently been awarded the best Unarmed Fancy Drill Team in the San Diego Unified School District as it has been for the past three years. As for the 2020-2021 school year, the team will be led by c/2d Lt. Rachelle Soriano and c/TSgt Christopher Villalobos. We hope to see you on our team!

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2018-2019 School Year:

Brigade Four Man: 2nd Place
Brigade Unarmed Exhibition Full Team: 1st Place
SCIDM Unarmed Full Team: 1st Place
Sweetwater Inspection/Drill/Exhibition: 1st Place
Golden Bear Novice Sweepstakes
