Color Guard is a ceremonious team consisting of 2 - 3 flag bearers and 2 riflemen. Throughout the school year, we present the colors and numerous community and school events. Our color guard was the number one color guard in SDUSD, placing first in the varsity category and 2nd in the 1st year category at the brigade drill competition. In the 2017-2018 school year the varsity color guard was chosen to compete at the national level in Daytona Florida. This year the team is led by c/2d Lt Kai Tomasyk and c/TSgt Maya Rudi, Whose goals are to prepare the team for success and keep the title as number on color guard in the brigade.

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2020-2021 School Year:

Color Guard Varsity (Brigade): 1st Place
Color Guard 1st Year (Brigade): 2nd Place
Color Guard Varsity (Sweetwater Drill Meet): 1st Place
Color Guard Varsity (Golden Bear West Coast Nationals): 3rd Place
