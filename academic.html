CA-935th's Academic Team is a close-knit group that trains to answer Jeopardy-style questions from a variety of different categories. They compete in two competitions throughout the year. The first one, the brigade competition, is held at high schools across San Diego, and CA-935th's cadets are currently the reigning champs! The second competition is the Joint Leadership and Academic Bowl (JLAB) nationals in Washington, D.C. The team has a variety of opportunities for recognition, including ribbons, medals, trophies, and Stillwell points for the unit. This year, c/2d Lt Kamryn Liu and c/TSgt Alyssa Zhou will be leading CA-935th's Academic Bowl team to the championship!

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2024-2025 School Year:

Brigade Round 1 (September 18) - 1st Place
Brigade Round 2 (October 9) - 1st Place
Brigade Round 3 (October 23) - 1st Place
Brigade Round 4 (November 13) - 1st Place
Placed Second in SDUSD Brigade Academic League
Both JLAB teams qualified for JLAB Level II
Qualified for JLAB National Academic Championship
Placed fourth out of top sixteen Air Force schools in the country
