CA-935th's Academic Team is a close-knit group that trains to answer Jeopardy-style questions from a variety of different categories. They compete in two competitions throughout the year. The first one, the brigade competition, is held at high schools across San Diego, and CA-935th's cadets are currently the reigning champs! The second competition is the Joint Leadership and Academic Bowl (JLAB) nationals in Washington, D.C. The team has a variety of opportunities for recognition, including ribbons, medals, trophies, and Stillwell points for the unit. This year, c/2d Lt Yoonah Lee and c/TSgt Kamryn Liu will be leading CA-935th's Academic Bowl team to the championship!

Below are some of our proudest achievements during the 2022-2023 School Year:

Brigade Round 1 (Sept 13) - 1st Place
Brigade Round 2 (October 11) - 1st Place
Brigade Round 3 (October 25) - 1st Place
Brigade Round 4 (November 15) - 1st Place

Top Academic Bowl Team in SDUSD Brigade

CA-935th had two teams of JLAB Level II Qualifiers advancing to the next level in the national championship
